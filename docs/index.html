WHO WE ARE?

Our vision is that we search to build a community of young leaders inspired by the curiosity, science and technology.

FIRST

For inspiration and recognition of science and technology.

It is an organization that help the young students to take control over the pencils to redact the future of their own story.

They generate a competition around the world, where more than 600K students, 300K coaches and advisors in more than 100 countries with more than 3600 yearly events where more than 50K robots are build in just 6 weeks to compete one with the other.

WHAT DO WE DO?

Our mission is to influenciate in others the sense of social conscious, curiosity and love for science; gained through competition, effort, passion and the fun between the members of the team.
